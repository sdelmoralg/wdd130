About Me

Samanta Del Moral

Hello everyone, my name is Samanta, and I'm originally from Mexico. I've been living in the US for about 13 years. Since moving to the US, I have lived in 5 states (Az, Tx, Ut, Fl, Va). We live in Virginia now, and I love it here; it has been my favorite place.

I met and married my husband eight years ago, and we have three little boys (6,4 and 1-year-old).

I'm pursuing a degree in Applied Technology. I feel very blessed to have the chance to study online because it gives me the freedom to choose my study hours.

I don't have many hobbies right now because of kids. Instead, I spend my day doing stuff with them and trying to keep the house clean. But I enjoy crafting and gardening, and I absolutely love to travel; last summer, we went on a two-month-long road trip and visited 20 National Parks! all while doing school at the same time!

I've been a member of the church since 2010, and it has been the best decision of my life!
